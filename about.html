About Me

Hi there! My name is Ryan Fritz and I'm a research assistant at the Laboratory of Computational Neuroimaging (LCN) working under Doug Greve, Adrian Dalca, and Bruce Fischl. I am interested in cognitive neuroscience and approaches to induce plasticity in the brain ranging from neuropharmacology to neurostimulation.
